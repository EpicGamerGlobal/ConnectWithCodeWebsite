Refund Policy

1. Full Refund
Students are eligible for a full refund if they withdraw from the course at least 7 days before the first session.

2. Partial Refund
A 50% refund is available if the student withdraws within the first two sessions. No refunds will be provided after the second session.

3. Exceptional Circumstances
Refunds for medical or family emergencies will be considered on a case-by-case basis. Documentation may be required.

4. Refund Request Process
To request a refund, email us at [your-email@example.com] with the subject line \"Refund Request\" and include the student\u2019s name, course enrolled, and reason for withdrawal.

5. Processing Time
Refunds will be processed within 14 business days of approval.

6. Non-Refundable Items
Course materials, such as handouts or recordings, are non-refundable.
